Welcome to NaeesWrites.

This is where I'll share my thoughts on technology, design, and everything in between. I've been meaning to start a blog for a while, and this is my attempt at doing just that.

## Why this blog?

There are a few reasons I'm starting this:

1. **To think out loud** — Writing helps me clarify my thoughts
2. **To document** — I'll inevitably forget things I've learned
3. **To connect** — Maybe someone else will find this useful

## What to expect

I plan to write about:

- Technology and software development
- Design and creativity
- Philosophy and ideas
- Random thoughts that don't fit elsewhere

No particular schedule — I'll write when I have something to say.

Thanks for stopping by. Feel free to [reach out](https://github.com/Naees) if you want to connect.
